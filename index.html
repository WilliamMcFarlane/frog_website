✨ Now we're doing a list of frog facts stuff ✨

  1. Frogs have ears. Male frogs have bigger ears than females. Their ears are just behind their eyes.
  2. Frogs ‘drink’ through their skin.
  3. There isn’t actually a biological difference between frogs and toads. Toads are just very warty frogs.
  4. Flying frogs can glide for 12 to 15 metres, as they have extremely effective webbed feet.
